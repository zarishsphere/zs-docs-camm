## 1. Purpose

This MOU establishes a framework of cooperation for the adoption and implementation of the ZarishSphere open-source digital health platform in [COUNTRY/PROGRAM NAME].

---

## 2. ZarishSphere Commitments

ZarishSphere commits to:

2.1 **Platform Access:** Provide full access to all ZarishSphere open-source repositories under Apache 2.0 licence with no usage fees, ever.

2.2 **Documentation:** Maintain comprehensive documentation, guides, and runbooks in English (with translation support).

2.3 **Community Support:** Respond to GitHub Issues and community questions within 5 business days.

2.4 **Technical Support:** Provide onboarding support through L1 (Foundation) stage.

2.5 **No Lock-in:** The platform uses only open standards (FHIR R5, OpenTofu, Kubernetes) and the adopter retains full rights to self-host and modify.

2.6 **Data Sovereignty:** Clinical data remains under the complete control and jurisdiction of Party B. ZarishSphere has no access to clinical data.

---

## 3. Party B Commitments

[COUNTRY/ORGANIZATION] commits to:

3.1 **Designated Focal Point:** Appoint a named focal point with authority to make implementation decisions.

3.2 **Community Participation:** Participate in the ZarishSphere community (GitHub, community calls) in good faith.

3.3 **Contribution Back:** Where possible, contribute country-specific forms, translations, and improvements back to the community under Apache 2.0.

3.4 **Attribution:** Credit ZarishSphere in publications, reports, and presentations about the platform.

3.5 **Responsible Use:** Use the platform in accordance with applicable national health laws, HIPAA/GDPR principles, and patient privacy standards.

---

## 4. Duration

This MOU is effective from the date of signing and continues indefinitely unless terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

---

## 5. Termination

Either party may terminate this MOU with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, Party B retains all rights to their data and their fork of the platform (Apache 2.0 licence).

---

## 6. No Financial Obligation

This MOU creates no financial obligation on either party. ZarishSphere is free, open-source software.

---

## 7. Governing Principles

This MOU is governed by the principles of open-source collaboration, health equity, and data sovereignty as defined in PHILOSOPHY.md.
