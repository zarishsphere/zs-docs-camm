# ZarishSphere Training Curriculum

> **Version:** 2.0.0 | **Updated:** 2026-03-24
> **Languages:** Available in EN, BN — request other languages from platform team

---

## Training Tracks by CAMM Level

### L1 Training Track — Pilot Staff (8 hours total)

**Audience:** All clinical staff at the pilot facility

| Module | Duration | Method | Who Delivers |
|--------|----------|--------|-------------|
| 1. ZarishSphere Introduction | 30 min | Presentation | Platform team |
| 2. Patient Registration | 2 hours | Hands-on | Focal point |
| 3. Encounter and Vitals Entry | 2 hours | Hands-on | Focal point |
| 4. Clinical Forms | 2 hours | Hands-on | Focal point |
| 5. Data Quality and Reporting | 1 hour | Presentation | Focal point |
| 6. Troubleshooting Basics | 30 min | Q&A | Focal point |

**Competency Assessment:**
Each staff member must successfully complete 5 supervised patient registrations and 5 encounters before training is certified complete.

---

### L2 Training Track — Super Users (16 hours total)

**Audience:** 2 country-level platform managers

| Module | Duration | Method |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Platform Architecture Overview | 2 hours | Presentation |
| Form Builder (no-code form creation) | 4 hours | Hands-on |
| User and Role Management | 2 hours | Hands-on |
| Facility Onboarding Process | 2 hours | Hands-on walkthrough |
| Indicator and Dashboard Configuration | 3 hours | Hands-on |
| Troubleshooting and Support | 2 hours | Case studies |
| DHIS2 Configuration | 1 hour | Walkthrough |

---

### L3 Training Track — IT Staff (24 hours total)

**Audience:** Country IT staff managing the platform

| Module | Duration | Topics |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Platform Architecture Deep Dive | 4 hours | Microservices, FHIR, PostgreSQL, NATS |
| Kubernetes Operations | 4 hours | kubectl, Argo CD, Helm |
| Database Management | 4 hours | PostgreSQL backups, TimescaleDB queries |
| Security Management | 4 hours | Keycloak, Vault, network policies |
| Monitoring and Alerting | 4 hours | Grafana, Prometheus, Loki |
| Incident Response | 4 hours | Runbooks, escalation, postmortems |

## Certification

After completing each track, the focal point reports completion in the country's STATUS.md file and the ZarishSphere Health Programs team issues a community certification badge.
