# CAMM L5 — Sovereignty Stage

> **Level:** 5 | **Name:** Sovereignty | **Duration:** Ongoing

---

## What This Level Is About

L5 is the ultimate goal. The country is fully self-sufficient. They manage their own infrastructure, train their own staff, handle their own incidents, and contribute back to the ZarishSphere community.

---

## Entry Gate

- L4 exit criteria all met ✅
- Country has in-house DevOps capability ✅
- Country has been managing their own operations for 6+ months ✅

---

## What Sovereignty Means

### Technical Sovereignty
- Country owns and manages their own Kubernetes cluster
- Country manages their own PostgreSQL, NATS, Valkey, Keycloak
- Country runs their own CI/CD with Argo CD
- Country can deploy updates without ZarishSphere core team

### Governance Sovereignty
- Country has permanent representation in ZarishSphere governance
- Country votes on RFCs that affect their program
- Country has one GitHub org owner account

### Content Sovereignty
- Country maintains their own clinical forms
- Country manages their own translations
- Country contributes forms and protocols back to `zs-content-*`

### Community Contribution
- Country staff present at global ZarishSphere meetups
- Country contributes at least 1 clinical form per quarter to the community
- Country mentors new countries reaching L0/L1

---

## Ongoing Responsibilities at L5

- Keep platform updated (Renovate manages dependencies automatically)
- Respond to security advisories within 72 hours
- Participate in quarterly community calls
- Submit annual impact report to `zs-docs-donor`
- Vote on RFCs affecting the platform

---

## Congratulations 🎉

Reaching L5 means this country has achieved what ZarishSphere was built for: a national health program that owns its digital health infrastructure, protects its health data sovereignty, and contributes to the global health community.
