# CAMM L4 — Maturity Stage

> **Level:** 4 | **Name:** Maturity | **Typical Duration:** 18–36 months

---

## What This Level Is About

L4 is national rollout. ZarishSphere is the national digital health platform for primary care. All EHR modules are active. Public health surveillance feeds the national EWARS. Donor reporting is fully automated.

---

## Entry Gate

- L3 exit criteria all met ✅
- National budget allocated for operations ✅

---

## Activities During L4

### Full Clinical Module Deployment
All modules active: patient, encounter, observation, medication, immunization, condition, procedure, allergy, appointment, document, care-plan, consent, nutrition, maternity, child-health, mental-health, communicable-disease, laboratory, pharmacy, radiology.

### Public Health Surveillance
- EWARS 24/7 automated outbreak detection
- Real-time disease surveillance dashboard
- Automatic outbreak notification to MoH
- Syndromic surveillance running on anonymised encounter data

### ERP Modules
- HRM: staff records, leave, performance
- Inventory: medical supply chain management
- Finance: donor fund tracking and expenditure reporting
- Procurement: purchase orders and vendor management

### Quality Improvement
- Monthly clinical audit workflows
- Indicator performance dashboards
- Peer facility comparison (anonymised)

---

## Exit Criteria (to advance to L5)

- [ ] >80% of target facilities live
- [ ] All clinical domains active
- [ ] EWARS fully automated
- [ ] National donor report automated
- [ ] Country team trained in basic platform operations
- [ ] Business continuity plan documented and tested
