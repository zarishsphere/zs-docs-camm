# Country Adoption Maturity Model (CAMM)

ZarishSphere's CAMM defines the journey from first contact with a country to full digital sovereignty.

## The 6 Levels

### L0 — Interest
**What happens:** Country engagement and needs assessment
**Gate to next level:** Signed Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)
**Who does the work:** Platform Lead (arwa-zarish)

### L1 — Foundation  
**What happens:** Fork the country distro template, set up facility registry, train first users
**Gate to next level:** At least 1 health facility live
**GitHub repos created:** `zs-distro-{country}`, `zs-infra-{country}`

### L2 — Adoption
**What happens:** Core clinical modules running, basic reporting to MOH
**Gate to next level:** 10+ facilities live, DHIS2 connected
**GitHub repos created:** Clinical service configs, form content repos

### L3 — Integration
**What happens:** National system bridges live (DHIS2, OpenMRS, etc.)
**Gate to next level:** MOH integration officially live
**GitHub repos created:** `zs-int-{country}-*` integration repos

### L4 — Maturity
**What happens:** Full EHR, analytics, public health surveillance running nationally
**Gate to next level:** National rollout complete

### L5 — Sovereignty
**What happens:** Country manages their own ZarishSphere deployment
**Gate:** Country has its own DevOps team contributing back to platform
